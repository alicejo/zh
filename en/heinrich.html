Mark Heinrich

(Dipl.-Ing.)

Patent Attorney

Mark Heinrich graduated from the Technical University Berlin with a diploma in Mechanical Engineering in the year 2012. Mark Heinrich specialized in product design engineering, machine tool systems and production technology.

He further gained international working experience in India and Morocco in the automotive industry and in the field of solar energy.
After his studies, Mark Heinrich worked as a development engineer mainly involved in designing machine tools for laser-based material processing. His major responsibilities covered the accomplishment of technological feasibility studies, the development, design, supervision of manufacturing and commissioning of laser machine tool systems and quality control during customer’s production process. In another company in the field of software development and automation technology, he was entrusted with the establishment of an industrial patent department. In this context, he also dealt intensively with computer-implemented inventions, both on the basis of conventional programming and artificial intelligence.

Until 2019 Mark Heinrich freelanced for the IP law firm Gulde & Partner. Since 2020 started working for ZIEBIG & HENGELHAUPT as a freelance patent attorney.
