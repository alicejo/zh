Yehan Feng

(Dipl.-Vwt.)

Chinese attorney at law

Yehan Feng graduated a master degree in economics at the Free university of Berlin. She also studied in London. Ms. Feng joined the IP law firm Gulde & Partner in 2005 and she works as the head of the Beijing Office representative office since 2008. She became a Chinese attorney at law and was called to the Chinese bar in 2016.
Ms. Feng advises Chinese clients with respect to patent-, trademark- and design prosecution in Europe and Germany. She also advises in non-prosecution matters such as patent litigation, the risk of companies entering the EU market; Freedom-to-Operate analyses; protective letters for exhibitions in Germany and the like.

Since 2018, she also works for the IP law firm ZIEBIG & HENGELHAUPT.

Practice Areas

  • German, European und Chinese patent, trademark, design prosecution
  • German and Chinese IP Law

Languages

Chinese, German, English
