Law Firm

The ZIEBIG & HENGELHAUPT partnership company is a full service IP law firm with a history stretching back over more than 25 years. The origins of the firm go back to 1993/1994 when Klaus Gulde, Marlene Ziebig and Jürgen Hengelhaupt established a patent agency practice in Berlin previously known as Gulde Hengelhaupt Ziebig & Schneider and built up a significant portfolio of German and international clients.
